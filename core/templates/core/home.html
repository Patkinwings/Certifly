About the CompTIA A+ Exam

The CompTIA A+ certification is an essential credential for IT professionals. It validates the foundational skills needed for a career in IT support and operations.

  • Core 1 (220-1101) focuses on mobile devices, networking, hardware, virtualization, and cloud computing.
  • Core 2 (220-1102) covers operating systems, security, software troubleshooting, and operational procedures.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the A+ certification last?

The CompTIA A+ certification is good for three years from the date of certification.

Do I need to take both Core 1 and Core 2 exams?

Yes, you need to pass both the Core 1 (220-1101) and Core 2 (220-1102) exams to earn the CompTIA A+ certification.
